# Ragu 

## Ingredients

- Chicken Stock
- Tomatoes
- Carrots
- Celery
- Onions
- Garlic
- Basil
- Oregano
- Parsley
- Paprika
- Black Pepper
- Browned Meat
- Mushrooms
- bay leaves
- thyme
- Red Wine

For Arrabbiata sauce, add lots of spicy red peppers. For Saltsa Kima, throw in Cinnamon, Cloves, Allspice, and Cumin.
