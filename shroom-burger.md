# Vegetarian Mushroom Burger Patties

## Ingredients

- Largest Portobello Mushrooms you can find
- Dale's Smokey Seasoning Sauce (Optional)
- Worcestershire Sauce
- Quebec Steak Seasoning
- Olive Oil
- Red Wine
- Garlic Paste
- Oregano
