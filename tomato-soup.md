# Tomato Soup

## Ingredients

- Tomatoes, lots of 'em
- Carrots
- Celery
- Onion
- Bay Leaves
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Italian Seasoning
- Chicken Stock
- Bacon
- Mushrooms
- Garlic
- Basil
- Parmesan Cheese
- Heavy Cream
- Black Pepper
- Cayenne
- Cumin (optional)
- Croutons
- olive oil

goes great with caesar salad; save the bacon for a grilled cheese on smoked chedder with date-tamarind chutney
